Tibs(ጥብስ)

Description

Tibs, a classic Ethiopian delight, offers bite-sized pieces of marinated and
seared meat, richly spiced with berbere, garlic, and ginger, creating a delectable dish bursting with aromatic flavors.

Ingredients for Tibs

  • 1 lb of beef or lamb, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons berbere spice (Ethiopian red chili spice blend)
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil for cooking

Steps to Make Tibs

  1. Start by selecting your preferred meat, such as beef or lamb, and cut it into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Marinate the meat with a blend of Ethiopian spices, including berbere (a red chili spice blend), garlic, ginger, and other optional seasonings.
  3. Allow the meat to marinate for at least 30 minutes to let the flavors infuse.
  4. Heat a skillet or pan over medium-high heat and add a touch of oil. Once hot, add the marinated meat to the pan.
  5. Sear the meat until it's browned on all sides, ensuring it's cooked to your desired level of doneness.
