Beyayinetu(በየአይነቱ)

Description

Beyayinetu, the Ethiopian vegetarian feast, presents a colorful platter of diverse dishes,
including lentils, split peas, and an array of vegetables, served on injera, providing a delightful and satisfying plant-based culinary journey.

Ingredients for Beyayinetu(በየአይነቱ)

  • 1 cup red lentils, cooked
  • 1 cup yellow split peas, cooked
  • 1 cup collard greens, chopped and cooked
  • 1 cup cabbage, shredded and cooked
  • 1 cup carrots, diced and cooked
  • 1 cup potatoes, diced and cooked
  • 1 cup beetroot, diced and cooked
  • 1 cup injera (Ethiopian sourdough flatbread)

Steps to Make Beyayinetu

  1. Prepare injera or acquire it from a local Ethiopian restaurant or store.
  2. Cook each vegetable separately until tender, using traditional Ethiopian spices like garlic, ginger, and berbere for added flavor.
  3. Arrange the cooked vegetables and lentils on a large platter, placing them on top of the injera.
  4. Serve additional injera on the side to scoop up the different dishes.
  5. Enjoy the vibrant and delicious flavors of Ethiopian Beyayinetu!
