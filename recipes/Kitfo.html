Kitfo(ክትፎ)

Description

Kitfo, a jewel in Ethiopian cuisine, is a flavorful dish featuring finely minced raw or lightly cooked
beef, blended with spices and spiced clarified butter, delivering a unique and savory taste experience.

Ingredients for Kitfo

  • 200g finely minced or ground raw beef (preferably lean)
  • 2 tablespoons niter kibbeh (spiced clarified butter)
  • 1 tablespoon mitmita (a spicy chili powder blend)
  • 1 teaspoon ground korarima (Ethiopian cardamom), optional
  • Salt to taste

Steps to Make Kitfo

  1. Ensure the raw beef is finely minced or ground, and if desired, you can lightly cook it for safety.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the minced beef with mitmita (spicy chili powder) and ground korarima (Ethiopian cardamom), if using.
  3. Heat the niter kibbeh (spiced clarified butter) in a pan until melted and aromatic.
  4. Pour the melted spiced butter over the seasoned minced beef and mix thoroughly until well combined.
  5. Season with salt according to your taste. Kitfo is traditionally served raw, but you can adjust the level of spiciness and doneness based on your preference.
